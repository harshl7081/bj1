Our Story

A legacy of brilliance since 1998 from Surat, Gujarat, India.

Established 1998

A Legacy of Brilliance

Established in the year 2005 in Surat (Gujarat, India), we "Beautiful Jewel" are a prominent manufacturer, trader, importer, exporter and supplier of premium quality range of Ladies Bangle, Ladies Earring, Ladies Ring, Ladies Bracelet, Ladies Pendant and Pendant Set.

We started our journey in 1998, and the company was officially registered in 2005. As a Sole Proprietorship (Individual) based company, we have grown from a vision to create the most exquisite real diamond jewelry into a globally recognized manufacturing and exporting house.

As per the current fashion trend, our jewelleries are well designed using high quality material and advanced machinery. Owing to their features like beautiful look, light weight, attractive pattern, eye-catching design and glossy finish, these jewelleries are highly appreciated among our clients.
