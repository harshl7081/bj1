Certified Natural Diamond Necklaces Online India

Elevate your presence with certified natural diamond necklaces from Beautiful Jewel — India's most trusted diamond jewellery manufacturer since 1998. Each necklace in our collection is a masterwork of design and precision, featuring GIA or IGI certified natural diamonds set in the finest precious metals to create pieces that transcend trends and become cherished heirlooms.

Our diamond necklace collection offers extraordinary variety. Our solitaire diamond pendants feature a single brilliant-cut stone suspended on a delicate chain — the epitome of understated luxury. For those seeking greater impact, our diamond pendant sets pair stunning neckpieces with matching earrings for a coordinated look. Our multi-row diamond necklaces layer multiple strands of diamonds for opulent, red-carpet-worthy drama, while our diamond chokers sit close to the neck for a contemporary, fashion-forward aesthetic.

Every diamond in our necklaces is hand-selected to meet our exacting standards. We work exclusively with natural, conflict-free diamonds graded between VS1 and VVS clarity with excellent to very good cut grades. Our gemologists ensure colour consistency across every stone in multi-diamond pieces — a critical factor that distinguishes luxury necklaces from ordinary ones. The result is a necklace where every diamond exhibits uniform brilliance and fire.

Our manufacturing process combines advanced CAD/CAM design technology with traditional hand-craftsmanship. Each necklace begins as a precise 3D model, ensuring perfect proportions and stone placement before any metal is cast. Our master setters then hand-place each diamond, selecting the optimal setting technique — prong, bezel, or invisible — to maximise beauty and security. Available in 14K, 18K, and 22K gold (yellow, white, and rose) as well as platinum, every necklace is hallmarked for metal purity.

Diamond Necklace Care & Occasion Guide

Diamond necklaces are perfect for weddings, anniversaries, milestone birthdays, and formal events. A solitaire pendant is ideal for daily wear, adding quiet luxury to any outfit. For care, store diamond necklaces flat to prevent chain kinking. Clean with a soft brush and mild soap solution. Avoid exposing to chlorine, harsh chemicals, or extreme heat. Have clasps inspected annually for security. Beautiful Jewel provides lifetime re-polishing and maintenance for all necklaces.
